About Us
Not your average extended auto warranty company. Seriously.
Pretzel is the next generation of extended auto warranties.
We’re rejecting what most people think of when they think “extended warranty,” and replacing high pressure sales tactics and off-the-charts marketing budgets with simple good business, honesty and kindness. We want to get you the coverage you need for your vehicle – nothing more, nothing less.
Here’s who we are.
Industry leaders
Pretzel was founded in {2020}. Our founding team has tons of experience in this industry, but just couldn’t stand by the scare tactics and shady maneuvers. So we got together and built Pretzel.
On a mission
Our mission is {mission statement/to bring flexible, affordable, transparent extended auto warranties to the people – and do it with heart}.
With a vision
We want {vision statement/to be the top extended auto warranty company in the United States, not because we’re the most aggressive but because we’re the best}.
And we’re making good on our promises.
Real protection
Unlike those other guys, we actually pay for legitimate claims. When you submit a claim, we’ll make the decision based on your policy, not our bottom line. And if your claim is denied, we’ll talk to you about why.
Real heart
Plus, when we say we want to do good for the world, we don’t just mean making your extended auto warranty something that makes you want to (hug your tires/leap with joy/kiss your leaky transmission) – although we’re working on that too. We’re putting our money where our mouth is.
Real good
Or, more specifically, where some of our favorite causes are. When we make money, we give a percentage of our profits to a select list of charities. Because, simply enough, we care.
