Pretzel offers three tiers of coverage, so you can choose the plan you need without having to explore a plan maze.
Unsalted (Basic)
$29.99
Best for:
  Budget-conscious buyers
  Daily drivers looking to get the basics covered
Includes:
  Protection for your vehicle’s vital parts that get the most wear and tear
  Your choice of certified mechanic
  24/7 roadside assistance
  Rental car reimbursements & trip interruption coverage up to ${XX} per {unit}
  Low monthly payments as long as you need coverage
  Easy, anytime cancellation
Does not include:
  Coverage for certain less vital car parts
The Works (Premium)
$129.99
Best for:
  Drivers who rely heavily on their car
  Those seeking the total package, complete coverage
Includes:
  Higher level of protection for all your vehicle’s vital parts – and some of the not-so-vital ones too
  Your choice of certified mechanic
  24/7 roadside assistance
  Lockout assistance and coverage if you run out of gas
  New tires up to ${XX}
  Rental car reimbursements & trip interruption coverage up to ${XX} per {unit}
  Accessible monthly payments as long as you need coverage
  Easy, anytime cancellation
Does not include:
  Nothing, it includes everything we offer. How cool is that?
Salted (Standard)
$79.99
Best for:
  Those seeking balance
  Robust coverage at a reasonable price
Includes:
  Coverage for all your vehicle’s vital parts
  Your choice of certified mechanic
  Total protection from sudden breakdowns
  24/7 roadside assistance
  Lockout assistance and coverage if you run out of gas
  Rental car reimbursements & trip interruption coverage up to ${XX} per {unit}
  Affordable monthly payments as long as you need coverage
  Easy, anytime cancellation
Does not include:
  A limited number of clearly listed excluded components
