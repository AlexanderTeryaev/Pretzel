Moving forward together
Pretzel is a tech-driven, 100% remote company looking for changemakers who are always ready to try new things. At Pretzel, we listen to our employees and make sure that everyone has a voice. Basically, we're seeking team members who have something to say. Our work environment disrupts the industry by prioritising kindness and flexible work models. If you have the drive and passion to create the future of extended auto warranties (yes, we know how that sounds), send us a resume and we'll get in touch.

Future-facing flexibility
In these unusual times, companies worldwide have adapted to remote work out of necessity (with mixed success). But we were born remote. We’ll stay remote when things change again someday. We want people who are interested in forward-thinking, technology-based solutions. Self-starters who look for new ways to solve old problems and give 110%, no matter what the task or where they’re working from (yes, it’s ok if it’s by the pool). We’ll make sure you have the tools you need to do your job from anywhere, you bring your drive to take Pretzel everywhere.
